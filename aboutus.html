About Us

Our Goals Here at Climate Clue

A Cleaner, Greener Future is within Reach.

We are a group of people dedicated to action against climate change. We, alongside over 140 organisations (including the National Trust, Women's Institute, Oxfam, and RSPB) are reaching across the UK to show our love for all the things we want to protect from climate change, and to ask politicians to put aside their differences and commit to doing whatever is necessary to protect them.

Together we can achieve so much and reach millions with campaigns and with you, our advocates. Already, so much progress has been made such as the UK becoming the first major economy to set a legally binding net zero target.
